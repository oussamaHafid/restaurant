Tangia Moroccan tangia is a traditional dish from Marrakech, made with meat (usually lamb or beef) and Moroccan spices such as cumin, coriander, and garlic. It is cooked in a clay pot called a "tanjara," where the ingredients are slow-cooked with oil and water for a long time. This slow cooking results in a rich flavor and very tender meat. Tangia is usually served with bread and is a popular dish for special occasions. $120
